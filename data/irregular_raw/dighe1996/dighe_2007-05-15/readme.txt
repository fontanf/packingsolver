Description: 
2D Irregular test problems (DIGHE) from DIGHE/JAKIELA (1996)
Jigsaw problems (with known optimum), constructed according to a sample layout in the paper Dighe and Jakiela (1996) by Eva Hopper
(Data sets: dighe1, dighe2)

References: 
Dighe, R, Jakiela, M.J., 1996, Solving Pattern Nesting Problems with Genetic Algorithms Eploying Task Decomposition and Contact Detection, 
Evolutionary Computation 3, 239-266
